D2 is a 24-hour, student-run hackathon at UC Davis emphasizing hacks that further social good, including environmental, medical, and social issues. D2 focuses on collaboration over competition, and matches designers with developers to create great projects.

D2 is a collaboration between the Davis Computer Science Club and PIXEL, the student-run clubs at UC Davis in the computer science and design disciplines, respectively.

Keep in mind the judging criteria:
1. Originality & uniqueness — has this need already been met?
2. Fulfillment of purpose — how well does your project accomplish its goal?
